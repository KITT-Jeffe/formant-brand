01 — Brand Positioning
Formant sees the physical world in spectra others don't monitor. The anomaly before the failure. The pattern before the incident.

V2 shifts the register from documentary to editorial. Same brand conviction — quieter confidence, more contemporary surface. The world it operates in is precise, fast, and technically literate.

Precise
Specific over general. Numbers over adjectives. One data point that matters over ten that don't.
Intriguing
Withholds just enough. Pulls you forward. The brand knows something. You want to find out what.
Contemporary
Not retrofuturism. Not steampunk tech. The present, very clearly seen.
Physical
Machines, operations, consequence. Things that exist in space and break when they fail.

04 — Photography Direction

V2 shifts from Hasselblad/Tri-X documentary to contemporary editorial — crisp, precise, slightly cool. Color photography where the color itself carries information. The machine as a precision instrument.
