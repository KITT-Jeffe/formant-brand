01 — Brand Positioning
The anomaly was always there. Formant was listening.

V7 applies Teenage Engineering restraint to the Formant brand system. Where V6 was bold and typographically aggressive, V7 withholds. Dark-dominant. Light-weight type at large scale. German-language labels as quiet misdirection. The spec grid borrows TE's product page density — a wall of capability stated plainly, without hierarchy. The brand idea is the same; the register is lower. More unsettling. More correct.

The grayscale gradient — the only decorative element — is the identity itself. Everything else earns its place or it's not there.

Withholding
Doesn't explain what it doesn't need to. Precision implies depth. You sense more beneath the surface.
