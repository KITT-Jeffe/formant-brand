01 — Brand Positioning
The warning before the warning.

V7 draws from industrial design — Braun's material honesty, the visual language of heavy machinery, the warm neutral palette of a working environment. Orange is not a choice for style. In industrial contexts orange is the pre-failure signal: not danger (red), not safe (green) — but the moment between them. Formant lives precisely there. The cream-warm palette grounds the brand in the physical world. This is not software. This is a system that understands consequence.
