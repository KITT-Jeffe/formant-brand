01 — Icon System

Operational iconography

24×24px grid. 1.5px stroke, square caps, miter joins. Each icon represents a concept from Formant's operational world — not generic UI. The system is spare: only draw what you need to say.

Construction principles

Grid: 24×24px · Optical area: 20×20px · Stroke: 1.5px · Caps: square · Joins: miter
Corner radius: 1px (structural), 0px (detail) · No fill icons at small sizes
Pixel-aligned at 1×, 2×, 3× · Export at 2× minimum
